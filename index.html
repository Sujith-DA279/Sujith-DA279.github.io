Business Intelligence & Data Analyst (BIDA) Certification

This certification demonstrates completion of CFI's Business Intelligence and Data Analysis® program. To achieve this certification, participants must complete 14 core courses and 3 elective courses on topics of business intelligence and data science. In total, this represents over 100 hours of coursework. In addition, learners pass a timed exam that incorporates realistic scenarios and data sets. Through the coursework and exams, learners apply tools like Power BI, Power Pivot, Power Query, Python, Excel, Tableau, and Azure Data Studio. All courses within this program incorporate hands-on scenario-based learning with examples taken from finance industry verticals.

LSE Data Analytics Career Accelerator

Completed the LSE Data Analytics Career Accelerator program, an intensive, hands-on data analytics program from London School of Economics, covering comprehensive aspects of business data analytics - from foundational SQL, Excel to advanced predictive modeling with Python.
